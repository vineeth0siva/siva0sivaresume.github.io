short explanation of project

Catering to the need for timely action, our AI model not only identifies leaf diseases for farmers but also enables administrators, such as agriculture officers, to proactively prevent disease spread. When a leaf is identified as diseased, the agricultureofficer(admin) can swiftly notify other farmers, facilitating a collaborative effort to protect crops and improve agricultural practices.
